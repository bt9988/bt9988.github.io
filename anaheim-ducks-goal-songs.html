Anaheim Ducks Goal Song

The current goal song for the NHL's Anaheim Ducks is "Coming For You" by The Offspring. This song has been in use since 2024.

Goal Song History

The Anaheim Ducks have used the following goal songs:

  • "Bro Hymn" by Pennywise (2005–2024)
  • "Kernkraft 400" by Zombie Nation (2005)
