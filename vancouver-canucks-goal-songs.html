Vancouver Canucks Goal Song

The current goal song for the NHL's Vancouver Canucks is "Don't You Forget About Me" by Simple Minds.

Goal Song History

The Vancouver Canucks have used the following goal songs:

  • "Ain't Talkin' 'bout Love" by Van Halen (2019-2022)
  • "Holiday" by Green Day (2011, 2018-2019)
  • "Elevation" by U2
  • "The Whip" by Locksley
  • Individual Goal Songs
