Chicago Blackhawks Goal Song

The current goal song for the NHL's Chicago Blackhawks is "Chelsea Dagger" by The Fratellis. This goal song has been in use since 2008.

Goal Song History

The Chicago Blackhawks have used the following goal songs:

  • "Song 2" by Blur
  • "Tick Tick Boom" by The Hives
  • "Crowd Chant" by Joe Satriani
  • Individual Goal Songs
