Colorado Avalanche Goal Song

The current goal song for the NHL's Colorado Avalanche is "Chase the Sun" by Planet Funk. This goal song has been in use since 2017.

Goal Song History

The Colorado Avalanche have used the following goal songs:

  • "Delirious" by Steve Aoki (2016-2017)
  • "Born To Rage" by Dada (2015-2016)
  • "Rock and Roll, Part 2 + The Whip" by Gary Glitter + Locksley (2011-2015)
  • "Song 2" by Blur (2006-2007)
  • "Rock and Roll, Part 2" by Gary Glitter (1996-2006, 2007-2011)
