Minnesota Wild Goal Song

The current goal song for the NHL's Minnesota Wild is "Shout" by The Isley Brothers. This goal song has been in use since 2022.

Goal Song History

The Minnesota Wild have used the following goal songs:

  • "Jump Around" by House of Pain (2021-2022)
  • "Let's Go Crazy" by Prince (2016-2018)
  • "Crowd Chant" by Joe Satriani (2000-2016, 2018-2020)
