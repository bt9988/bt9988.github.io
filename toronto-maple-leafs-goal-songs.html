Toronto Maple Leafs Goal Song

The current goal songs for the NHL's Toronto Maple Leafs are "Düp Düp" by Mickie Krause and "Panama" by Van Halen.

Goal Song History

The Toronto Maple Leafs have used the following goal songs:

  • "Let's Shake" by Teenage Head
  • "Pursuit of Happiness (Steve Aoki Remix)" by Kid Cudi
  • "Best Day of My Life" by American Authors
  • "Turbulence" by Laidback Luke & Steve Aoki feat. Lil Jon
  • "You Make My Dreams" by Hall & Oates
