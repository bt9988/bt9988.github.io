Dallas Stars Goal Song

The current goal song for the NHL's Dallas Stars is "Puck Off" by Pantera.

Goal Song History

The Dallas Stars have used the following goal songs:

  • "The Whip" by Locksley (2011-2013)
  • "Rock and Roll, Part 2" by Gary Glitter (1992-2011)
