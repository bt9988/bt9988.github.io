Philadelphia Flyers Goal Song

The current goal song for the NHL's Philadelphia Flyers is "Ain’t Talkin' 'bout Love" by Van Halen.

Goal Song History

The Philadelphia Flyers have used the following goal songs:

  • "Booyah" by Showtek ft. We Are Loud & Sonny Wilson
  • "Fluxland" by XL
  • "Rock and Roll, Part 2" by Gary Glitter
  • "Bro Hymn" by Pennywise
  • "Feel the Shake" by Jetboy
  • "Ain’t Talkin' 'bout Love" by Van Halen
  • "My Songs Know What You Did in the Dark (Light Em’ Up)" by Fall Out Boy
  • "Maria (I like it Loud)" by Scooter
