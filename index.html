Understanding Ceiling Fan Functions

How Ceiling Fans Work

Ceiling fans are a great way to regulate temperature in your home efficiently. By changing the direction of the fan blades, you can either cool down a room or help distribute heat evenly.

Cooling Mode

In the summer, set your ceiling fan to rotate counterclockwise. This direction creates a breeze that pushes cool air down, making the room feel cooler.

Heating Mode

In the winter, set your ceiling fan to rotate clockwise. This direction pulls cool air up, which helps push the warm air near the ceiling down into the room, distributing heat more evenly.
