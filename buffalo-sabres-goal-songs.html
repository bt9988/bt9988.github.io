Buffalo Sabres Goal Song

The current goal songs for the NHL's Buffalo Sabres are "Kickstart My Heart" by Mötley Crüe (Blue and Gold jerseys) and "Song 2" by Blur (Black and Red jerseys jerseys). This goal song has been in use since 2008.
